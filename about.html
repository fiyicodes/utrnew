Who we are ?

Utricle Studios is an elite film studio with exceptional technical, creative and production prowess leading the African continent in animation production and intriguing story telling At Utricle, we interpret ideas and concepts into captivating stories through our mastery of explainer videos, storyboard illustrations, animation, and live-action. We tell stories the Utricle way; captivating, creative and informative. From a scribble on paper to the most visually engaging images on the screen, we continue to push the frontiers of creativity in technology and content creation for animation and live action to appeal to audiences of all ages.

We pride ourselves in creating innovative, new generation animated feature films, disruptive contents and other related products. Our ever-evolving production pipeline catalysed by the integration of propriety technology with world class creative talents ensures our content have the audience on the edge of their seats with an experience that leaves them wanting more. For us, we believe that even the most complex ideas can be interpreted into the most intriguing stories.
